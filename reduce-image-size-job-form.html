Why Reduce Image Size for Job Forms?

  • Many job application portals limit the maximum upload size for images.
  • Smaller images upload faster and reduce data usage.
  • Compressing images helps maintain form submission speed and reduces server load.

How to Keep Image Quality While Compressing
