What Are Meta Tags?

Meta tags are snippets of HTML code placed in the head section of your website. They provide essential information to search engines and social media platforms. The most important ones are:

  • Meta Title: Appears as the clickable link in search engine results.
  • Meta Description: A brief summary of the page’s content, shown under the title.
  • Meta Keywords: A list of relevant keywords (less used by modern search engines).
