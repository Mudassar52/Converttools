🌐 What Is an IP Address?

An IP address (Internet Protocol address) is a unique identifier assigned to your device when connected to the internet. It’s how websites and online services recognize and communicate with you.

  • IPv4: Most common format, looks like 192.168.1.1
  • IPv6: Newer, more secure, and longer (e.g., 2001:0db8::1)

🛡 Is It Safe to Share My IP?

Your public IP is visible to every website you visit. While it's generally safe, be cautious when sharing it publicly. For extra privacy, use a VPN or proxy to mask your original IP address.
