🔍 What is a Robots.txt File?

A robots.txt file is a standard used by websites to communicate with web crawlers (bots). It tells search engines which parts of your website can or cannot be indexed. This helps with:

  • Improving crawl efficiency
  • Protecting sensitive content
  • Managing duplicate content
  • Directing bots to important resources
